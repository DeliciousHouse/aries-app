# Governance

Aries AI is maintained by Sugar & Leather, LLC.

## Maintainers

Maintainers decide what is merged, released, deprecated, or rejected.

## Decision Rules

Maintainers prioritize:

1. Security
2. Tenant isolation
3. Runtime stability
4. Clear self-hosting
5. Compatibility with Aries Cloud / commercial support
6. Contributor experience

## Maintainer Rights

Maintainers may close issues or PRs that are:

- unsafe
- out of scope
- unmaintainable
- hostile
- duplicative
- inconsistent with project direction

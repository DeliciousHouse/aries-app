# Acceptable Use Policy

Aries AI is software for authorized, consent-based social content operations.
Deployments of Aries AI must comply with all applicable laws and with the terms
of every third-party platform they connect to.

Aries AI must not be used to:

- send unsolicited bulk messages or contact users without their consent
- impersonate people or organizations
- circumvent or evade the rules of connected platforms
- obtain or misuse credentials or OAuth tokens that the operator is not authorized to hold
- publish content on a person's or organization's behalf without their authorization
- produce synthetic media that is presented deceptively or without disclosure required by law or platform policy
- harass, threaten, or single out individuals
- run political persuasion campaigns without prior legal and compliance review
- collect data from platforms in violation of applicable terms of service or law

Maintainers may decline to support deployments that violate this policy.

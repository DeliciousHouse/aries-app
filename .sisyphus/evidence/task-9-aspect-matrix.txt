Matrix encoded in backend/social-content/aspect-matrix.ts
---------------------------------------------------------
  instagram + single_image  -> 4:5     (portrait feed crop, strictest)
  instagram + carousel      -> 1:1
  instagram + link_card     -> 1.91:1
  instagram + video         -> 9:16
  meta      + single_image  -> 1:1
  meta      + carousel      -> 1:1
  meta      + link_card     -> 1.91:1  (Open Graph link card)
  meta      + video         -> 9:16

resolveDominantImageChannel(channels): when an image media_request bundles
multiple channels, Instagram wins on a tie because 4:5 center-crops cleanly
to Meta 1:1, but 1:1 cannot be expanded to 4:5 without generated bleed.
